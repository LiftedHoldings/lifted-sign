## The two ways to use Lifted Sign

- **Self-host (free, AGPL-3.0).** Run the software on your own infrastructure. You handle
  the server, database, email, and TLS. There are **no usage limits, no volume policing,
  and no fees** from us — the license is the only agreement.
- **Hosted ($29.99/month).** We run all the infrastructure — updates, backups, email
  delivery, storage, and TLS. You get a ready-to-use account with nothing to operate.

## Fair use (hosted only)

The hosted plan is priced and provisioned for the document volume of a **typical small
business**. To keep the shared service fast and sustainable for everyone, we reserve the
right to:

- **suspend or rate-limit** a hosted account, or
- **require an upgrade to a higher-priced plan,**

when an account's usage **appears abusive** or **materially exceeds ordinary
small-business needs** — for example, sustained high-volume automated sending,
resale/bulk-processing on behalf of many third parties, or activity that degrades service
for other customers.

Where practical we will give notice and a chance to move to an appropriate plan before
suspending an account. Suspension for clear abuse, fraud, or activity that threatens the
service or its users may be immediate.

## No lock-in

Because the software is open source, you are never trapped on the hosted plan. If your
volume outgrows it — or you would simply rather run it yourself — you can
[self-host](./self-hosting.md) the identical software at any time, at no license cost.
