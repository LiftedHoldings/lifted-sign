01 What we collect

We collect only what we need to run the signing service. That falls into a few clear buckets:

Account information

  • Your email and name, so we can create your account, address your envelopes, and reach you about documents you send or receive.
  • Authentication data — you sign in with Google or with an email and password. If you enable it, we also store a phone number for optional SMS two-factor authentication.

Documents you upload

  • The PDFs and files you upload to send for signature, the fields you place on them, and the finished, executed documents.

Signer records

To make an electronic signature enforceable, each envelope carries an audit trail. For every signer we record:

  • Name and email address of the signer.
  • IP address and timestamps for key events — when the document was opened, consented to, and signed.
  • ESIGN / UETA consent records — proof that each signer agreed to sign electronically before the first field was completed.

We do not collect payment card details during beta. Lifted Sign is free with no card required. If we introduce paid plans in the future, any billing information would be handled by a payment processor — never stored on our servers in full.

02 How we use it

We use the information above strictly to deliver and protect the signing service:

  • Deliver the service — build and route your envelopes, present the signing experience to each party, and return the finished document.
  • Seal and certify — freeze each completed document, apply an AES-256 / PAdES seal, and generate a Certificate of Completion with a SHA-256 fingerprint and the full audit trail.
  • Security and integrity — verify identity at sign-in, detect abuse, and keep a tamper-evident record of what was signed and by whom.
  • Communicate with you — send signing requests, completion notices, and essential account or service messages.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your documents to train advertising or unrelated machine-learning models.

03 Sharing & subprocessors

Your documents are private to you and the people you send them to. We share information only in these limited ways:

  • With the signers you choose. Each party you add to an envelope can view the document they are asked to sign and the record of who has signed it.
  • With service providers (subprocessors) that help us run the platform, listed below. They may only process data on our instructions and to provide their service.
  • When legally required — to comply with a valid legal request, or to protect the rights, safety, and integrity of our users and the service.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to anyone.

04 Data retention

We keep your account information and documents for as long as your account is active, so your signed records stay available to you. Executed documents and their Certificates of Completion are retained as part of the durable, tamper-evident record of an agreement.

When you delete a document or close your account, we remove the associated data from our active systems. Residual copies may persist briefly in encrypted backups before they age out on our normal backup cycle, and we may retain limited records where the law requires it.

05 Security

Protecting signed records is the core of what we do.

  • Encryption in transit — all traffic to Lifted Sign is served over TLS.
  • Encryption at rest — documents and signatures are encrypted in storage.
  • Tamper-evident seal — when the last field is signed, the document is frozen, AES-256 / PAdES sealed, and fingerprinted with a SHA-256 hash recorded on the Certificate of Completion. Alter one byte and the fingerprint no longer matches — proof the record is exactly what everyone agreed to.
  • Access controls — sign-in via Google or email and password, with optional SMS two-factor authentication.

No online service can promise perfect security, and we don't. What we promise is honest engineering: encryption in transit and at rest, a tamper-evident seal, and no fabricated certifications we don't hold.
